My Story

I am Fei, a new product designer and UX/UI enthusiast with the extensive knowledge in corporate governance, risk management and controls. I have a diverse range of work experiences in the Financial Service industry prior to design work, holding positions including a Senior Risk Management Director at CIBC, an AVP of Internal Audit at Manulife and a Senior Audit Manager at KPMG.

Risk management expert and product designer: two career paths could not be more different, pivoting from one to the other in the space of 10 months seems impossible - but that’s exactly what I did.

I was starting my own Risk Management consulting business a year ago, and needed a web development / designer to launch my online presence with the custom risk assessment tool I had created. After a few months searching unsuccessfully for someone who shares my vision. I decided to learn UX/UI and create website on my own terms. The core elements of product design seemed to align with my passion for creativity, problem solving and analytical thinking. I enrolled in a UX/UI Boot Camp program at University of Toronto. That is how I got here.

15+ years working experience in Financial Services industry, with an education background of B.A. in Accounting and MBA in Finance and Investment, now a passionate product designer and a UX/UI lover. What more can you ask for?

My goal as a designer is to create engaging, functional, and accessible product which look professional and function intuitively for the target users.

I am constantly in the pursuit of knowledge and improving anything that I set my mind to.

Aside from all this love to travel (30 countries and counting), I am also a foodie and enjoy exploring different cuisines.

I was born in Shanghai, completed my college education in Switzerland, obtained my MBA degree, CPA license and worked in New York City, became a Chartered Accountant in Canada, now I am based in Toronto.

Thanks for stopping by. Resume available upon request.
